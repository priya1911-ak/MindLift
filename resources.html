Self-Help & Resources

Tools and techniques to support your daily well-being.

Guided Breathing

Take a moment for yourself. Follow the circle to regulate your breath.
Breathe in as it expands, out as it shrinks.

Inhale

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique

Use this when you feel overwhelmed or anxious to bring yourself back to the present.

5. See

Acknowledge 5 things you see around you. Maybe a bird, a plant, or a spot on the wall.

4. Touch

Acknowledge 4 things you can touch. Your hair, a chair, or the ground under your feet.

3. Hear

Acknowledge 3 things you hear. External sounds like traffic or the hum of a fridge.

2. Smell

Acknowledge 2 things you can smell. Coffee, old books, or even the scent of the air.

1. Taste

Acknowledge 1 thing you can taste. What does your mouth taste like? Or think of a favorite food.

Daily Self-Care Checklist

Small habits can make a big difference over time. Try to check these off every day.

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water
  • Get 15 minutes of sunlight
  • Practice 5 minutes of deep breathing
  • Limit social media to 30 minutes
  • Write down one thing you're grateful for
  • Move your body (stretching counts!)
